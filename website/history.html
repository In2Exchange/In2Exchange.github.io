school history

K. C. M. Academy was founded in 2001 and initially catered pre-school students in preparing them in a school setting.

In the year 2005, the institution was recognized by the Department of Education and also in the same year, the school transferred to its new modern building to accommodate other grade levels and expand its services for the students.

Through the help of their dedicated and competitive teachers and personnel, and with the support of compassionate parents, the school continued to produce quality education which guides the institution in forming outstanding and competitive students.
